About

We cater an elegant and interesting cuisine with a sophisticated Indian menu
Soul Curry is serving amazing Indian cuisine with authintic flavours for lunch and dinner. Start off with some traditional Onion Bhaji, Samosa or Aloo Bonda. From there, you can explore Vegetarian Galore, or the many dishes with chicken, lamb, beef and seafood. Our Tandoor oven breads are sinful, and plenty of accompaniments allow you to add even more delicious flavour. If you have room after dinner, our menu at Soul Curry is something you truly don't want to miss out on. Your taste buds deserve our takeaway tonight. We are dedicated to excellent customer service. Our excellent chefs unite to create the traditional Indian dishes from all corners of India.
Sour curry is a Perfect Place for ordering foods for Functions and large parties, Business Conferences, Wedding Ceremonies and Birthday Parties.
