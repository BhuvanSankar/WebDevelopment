ABOUT

 

Kiboko has been entrusted by Australian travelers since 1986. We started off as a family business, then connected with some friends in Africa and developed some tight relationships with a couple of different driver companies in Kenya and South Africa. We specialised in organizing safaris in the African savanna. We have headquarters in Brisbane and, since we cooperates with African companies to plan and operate trips, we also have offices in Cape Town, South Africa, and Nairobi, Kenya.
